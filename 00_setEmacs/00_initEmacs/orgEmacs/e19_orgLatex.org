** org PDF Processing

This customises LaTeX export to run pdflatex, biber and then pdflatex. Needed for citations.

  (setq org-latex-pdf-process
            '("pdflatex -interaction nonstopmode -output-directory %o %f"
              "bibtex %b"
              "pdflatex -interaction nonstopmode -output-directory %o %f"
              "pdflatex -interaction nonstopmode -output-directory %o %f"))
